Frequently Asked Questions

What is this?

This tool helps determine if a phrase contains an i, L, or the number 1. This tool was originally created to help IT professionals read frequently randomized local administrator passwords. However, anyone can use it! If you regularly need to tell differences between similar characters like I do, then you're in the right place.

How do I use this tool?

This tool is easy to use! Simply paste your phrase into the text box above and click 'Submit'! It's that easy! Your password will be displayed underneath where it was input. Your password will be displayed in an easy-to-read Consolas font. This style of font easily distinguishes between i and l characters. To distinguish number characters such as '1', numbers are highlighted red.

What happens to my data?

This website does not collect or sell any data regarding the user. The only cookie that is used simply keeps track of the last theme that was selected. We will never collect your passwords or any other metrics about you.
